Important Points to Mind While Exchanging Gift Cards for Cash

Author cardnoshPosted on April 12, 2019May 3, 2019

While the gift cards can be one of the more preferred presents and gifts nowadays, there are instances where you may not be able to use them. For instance, you may get a gift card of a store that does not have the inventory you like. In other cases, you may have a preference for particular online stores. Because returning the gift cards may not be a viable option, the best way to utilise them before they get to be redundant is to exchange them for cash.

There are trustable and reputed online website and businesses that can offer you the best value for your gift card. These firms may a deal in Gift cards of Amazon, Sephora, Google Play, Walmart, Apple, and of other leading firms. To get the best value and to make a reliable and hassle-free exchange, it is important that you consider certain important points when you want to exchange gift cards for cash.

You will not get the full value for your card

While you may easily get up to 92% of the gift card value in the form of cash, you may not get a hundred percent value of it. Because all companies dealing in gift card levy a small fee on the transaction, the value of your gift card gets marginally used when you exchange it. It is good to evaluate a card, analyse its usefulness, and sell it at the earliest possible time, when you are not going to use it. This will bring to the highest value for the card. Selling at reputed and trustworthy online portals may also provide you with a better deal.

Exchange your card at reputed websites

If you wish to trade and exchange gift cards for cash, you should see to it that the platform is registered under the Nigeria Corporate Affairs Commission. All authentic firms in this business are registered with the government agency. The firm will also provide to you the registration number, which reflects that it is truly recognised and certified to trade in the online/physical gift cards. The more reliable print and digital platforms in Nigeria including Techpoint Africa, Dailytrust Newspapers, and Information Nigeria among others will also recommend a trustworthy portal.

Dealing in all kinds of cards

See to it that your chosen company deals in all kinds of gift cards, including the Amazon, Google Play, Apple, Target, JC Penny, Steam, Sephora, and other cards. The leading platforms may also trade in other items like RMB and bitcoins. Such a platform is often more reliable, has better customer service, and has a large customer base as well. You can indulge in a long term partnership with them as well.

Choosing the right website will help you get the most value for your card, and will bring to you a hassle-free gift card exchange experience that may be over in less than 5 minutes. Do not compromise on the important aspects, to get the best deal for you.
